How Does it Work?

Vetting

We work with subject matter experts to determine the right projects to fund

There are many different projects and laboratories doing promising research. We diligently make sure we are funding the most impactful projects we can to further the overarching goals of each research area.

Distillation

We condense and illustrate the core concepts of the research

Research can be complex and difficult to understand, even for scientists in the field. We boil down the concepts into interesting and interactive demonstrations so you can better understand the research you’re contributing to!
