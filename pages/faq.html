Frequently Asked Questions

How do you decide what kinds of research to fund?

We're obsessed with spurring the next wave of human progress as fast as possible - ensuring humans live longer, healthier, and happier lives. This includes improving neurodegenerative disease therapies, ensuring sustainable living, and understanding the inner workings of the brain.

There are many companies and foundations addressing various different areas like renewable energy & space travel. We are putting our energy into fundamental neuroscience research - which we feel is underfunded and underrepresented. We are constantly on the lookout for more research areas that fit our criteria.

How do you pick which labs you partner with?

We're all former academic researchers with backgrounds in computer science, neuroscience, biomedical engineering, and mechanical engineering. We evaluate the mission behind a P.I.'s work, their potential impact on humanity's scientific understanding, and their ability to execute on projects. We also are building a board of scientific advisors with decades of experience in longevity tech and neuroscience.

If you're interested in being featured on our site, get in touch with us at founders@silkswap.com.

Can I donate to specific labs or projects?

Currently we only allow funding for specific labs and projects at especially high contributions. Here's why:

Today, the NIH requires P.I.'s to explain the projects they'll be conducting in lengthy detail (80-100 pages) before getting funding. This has two negative impacts: it wastes lots of valuable research time, and P.I.s stick to safe projects to guarantee funding. We want to give money directly to a P.I., so they can feel confident in taking on any projects that might be riskier but more impactful.

If you love a specific lab or project enough that you're only interested in donating to them, we'd love to hear about it! We're still working on adding this functionality to our site, but in the meantime, please get in touch with us at founders@silkswap.com.
