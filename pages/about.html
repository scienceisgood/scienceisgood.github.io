About Us

Axon was founded by former academic researchers frustrated at the inefficiency of basic scientific research are. Professors collectively spend millions of hours a year writing 100-page applications for grant money, of which only 10-15% are accepted. This is only a symptom of a larger problem: the research community is bogged down by bureaucracy that limits the rate at which labs can make progress.

Our Mission

Solutions to the biggest challenges that face humanity today, such as aging and hunger, are still locked up in research labs across the country. Millions of dollars flow to biotechnology companies, even though basic scientific research is slow, opaque, and starved of funding. Axon's mission is to open up scientific research and make it accessible to everyone, in order to move science forward faster.

Our Goals

Our goals are lofty but simple: we want to bring the science of the 2040s to the present day as soon as possible. This includes ending aging-related illnesses and accelerating a basic understanding of the brain's inner workings. To create fundamental scientific breakthroughs, we will first demystify research and generate new funding methods to provide labs with the resources they need.
